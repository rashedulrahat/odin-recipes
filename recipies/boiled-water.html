How to boil water

Description:

Boiling water - the ancient art mastered by only the bravest kitchen warriors. This recipe transforms ordinary tap water into a bubbling, steaming masterpiece. Whether you're cooking noodles, making tea, or proving to your friends that you can cook something, this legendary recipe is your first step toward kitchen domination.

Key Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (or more) of water
  • 1 pot with unmatched courage
  • 1 stove that actually works
  • Optional: An experienced wife
  • A Little Passion

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Battlefield

    Place your pot proudly on the stove like a king placing his crown. Make sure it's empty. If it's not… maybe wash it. No shame.

  2. Add the Magical Liquid

    Pour water into the pot.
    Tip: If you miss the pot and pour on the stove - you have unlocked the "Try Again" achievement.

  3. Turn Up the Heat

    Turn the stove knob to high.
    Warning: This step separates the beginners from the legends.

  4. Wait Like a Hero

    Stare at the pot.
    Then stare more.
    Then lose patience.
    Then suddenly - BOOM - bubbles! Steam! Drama!

  5. Celebrate the Boil

    Once the water is bubbling like a volcano with anger issues, congratulations - you've officially boiled water.

  6. Turn Off the Heat

    Don't forget this part, unless you enjoy chaos.

  7. Use It or Flex It

    Use your freshly boiled water for tea, noodles, or just brag to your friends that you “made something”.

  8. If you are married and don't have kitchen experience, it is safe to stay out of the kitchen.
